Cybertruck

The Cybertruck, is an innovative electric pickup truck that defies traditional automotive design norms. Its futuristic, angular exterior is constructed from ultra-hard 30X cold-rolled stainless steel, making it highly durable and resistant to dents and damage.

Specifications

  • Range: 500 miles on a single charge
  • Acceleration: Achieving zero to 60 mph in under 2.9 seconds
  • Autonomy: Lane-keeping, adaptive cruise control, traffic-aware cruise control, and Full Self-Driving (FSD)
